Some timelines of MS Dhoni

  • 1981 : Born to a humble family.
  • 1995 : Sent to play cricket for a local club by his football coach.
  • 1995 : Commando club offered him a 3 year contract.
  • 1998 : Selected for under-19 Bihar team.
  • 2000 : Maiden first class century against Bengal.
  • 2001-2003 : Worked as TTE at Kharagpur Railway Station.
  • 2003 : Discovered by BCCI's small-town talent-spotting initiative.
  • 2003 : Picked for India-A squad.
  • 2004 : Picked for Indian squad.
  • 2007 : Became T-20 and ODI captain.
  • 2007 : Led India to win T-20 World Cup.
  • 2008 : Awarded Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna award.
  • 2009 : Best ODI batsman of the year.
  • 2010 : Married Sakshi Yadav.
  • 2011 : Led India to win ODI World Cup.
  • 2014 : Retired from Test cricket.
  • 2016 : Became 2nd most succesful ODI captain.
